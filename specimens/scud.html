Scud or Side Swimmer

Sensitivity Rating Very Tolerant
Phylum Arthropoda
Class Crustacea
Order Amphipoda
Family Eight families
Size 5-25mm long
Habitat Typically along the edges of streams; large numbers in the weedy habitat of wetlands; occasionally in ponds
Diet Scavenger – any dead animals and aquatic vegetation
Features Small, shrimp-like and fast-swimming; seven pairs of legs, the first two adapted for grasping; also known as freshwater sandhoppers
