Flatworm

Sensitivity Rating Very Tolerant
Phylum Platyhelminthes
Class Turbellaria
Order Tricladida
Genus Seven genera
Size Up to 30mm
Habitat Substrate or the underside of the water surface film – in springs, ponds, brooks, ditches, marshes and lakes
Diet Carnivorous – soft or decomposing animal matter
Features Elongated body – flattened or cylindrical; dark eyes within white spots that can often be seen; blind gut that allows them to feed and remove waste from the same opening
