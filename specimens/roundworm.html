Roundworm

Sensitivity Rating Very Tolerant
Phylum Nematoda
Orders Dorylaimida, Mononchida, Monhysterida, Tripylida
Size Up to 12mm
Habitat Substrata of inland water – either on or just above its surface, or burrowed in shallowly. May be found in large numbers in organically polluted waters
Diet Omnivorous – all manner of organic matter, both dead and alive
Features Thin, elongated, and cylindrical in cross section; posterior end tapers to a point; unlike segmented worms (Oligochaets) as they have no hairs or setae (needle-like structures); also called nematodes
