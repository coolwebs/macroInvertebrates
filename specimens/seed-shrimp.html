Seed Shrimp

Sensitivity Rating Tolerant
Phylum Arthropoda
Class Ostracoda
Order Ostracoda
Family Eleven families
Size 0.25-6mm
Habitat Still to flowing, fresh or saline waters around shallow, weedy habitats. Mostly benthic
Diet Herbivorous/detritivorous filter feeder. Filter the water for plants, plankton and rotting materials to feed on
Features Entire body enclosed by two valves of the carapace. Propel themselves with an assortment of legs and antennae, which are thrashed around in the gap between the two valves.

Gooderham. J. & Tsyrlin. E., (2002).
