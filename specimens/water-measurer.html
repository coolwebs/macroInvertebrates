Water Measurer

Sensitivity Rating Tolerant
Phylum Arthropoda
Class Insecta
Order Hemiptera
Family Hydrometridae
Genus Hydrometra
Size Up to 150mm
Habitat Among emergent vegetation at the edge of the still waters
Diet Carnivorous – small animals associated with the surface film of water
Features Long and very slender body; head as long as the thorax; long legs and feet (tarsi), which assists it to travel across the water surface using surface tension
