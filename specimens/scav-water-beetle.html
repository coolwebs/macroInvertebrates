Scavenger Water Beetle

Sensitivity Rating Very Tolerant
Phylum Arthropoda
Class Insecta
Order Coleoptera
Family Hydropilidae
Size Adult: Less than 1mm to over 40mm.
Larva: up to 8mm
Habitat Adult: still waters or quiet areas of flowing water.
Larva: among decaying plant material at the margins of the still waters or quiet areas of flowing waters
Diet Adult: herbivorous (collectors, gatherers) – plant material.
Larva: carnivorous – small aquatic animals
Features Adult: dark or dull coloured with a smooth oval shape; antennae with seven to nine segments, the last three to five segments wider than the rest.
Larva: generally slender and flat; well-developed mouth parts and may have gills on abdomen
