Water Flea

Sensitivity Rating Tolerant
Phylum Arthropoda
Class Branchiopoda
Order Cladocera
Family Seven Families
Genus Over 40 genera in Australia
Size Up to 10mm
Habitat Some species are benthic (live on/in the bed of a water body), while some reside in the sheltered weedy areas of still or slow-flowing fresh water.
Diet Various diet depending on the species: some feed on decomposing plant and animal material, some on plankton and others only eat plants.
Features Have a two shelled body shield that encloses their body and legs but not the head. Movement is started by flicking their antennae, resulting in a flicking/jerking motion.

Gooderham. J. & Tsyrlin. E., (2002)
The waterbug book: a guide to freshwater macroinvertebrates
