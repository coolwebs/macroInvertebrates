Freshwater Snail

Sensitivity Rating Very Tolerant
Phylum Mollusca
Class Gastropoda
Family Twelve families
Size 3 – 65mm long
Habitat Among weeds and stones in slow and still sections of the creek
Diet Herbivorous – algae from the surface of rocks and plants
Features Coiled or limpet-like shell
