Water Boatman

Sensitivity Rating Very Tolerant
Phylum Arthropoda
Class Insecta
Order Hemiptera
Family Corixidae
Size Up to 10mm
Habitat Still and slow-flowing pools; on streambeds
Diet Omnivorous – algae or other small organisms
Features Long, flat, oval-shaped bodies; long, oar-like hind legs
