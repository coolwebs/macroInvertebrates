Rifle Beetle

Sensitivity Rating Sensitive
Phylum Arthropoda
Class Insecta
Order Coleoptera
Family Elmidae
Size Adult: up to 5mm.
Larva: up to 6mm.
Habitat Both adults and larvae are aquatic; stones, logs, or debris in fast-flowing sections of streams; prefer cool, high-oxygen water typical of upland streams
Diet Herbivorous – adults and larvae feed on plant material
Features Adult: long tarsal claws and long, slender antennae.
Larva: cylindrical bodies; generally brown or dark-coloured; gills extend from a chamber at the point of the last abdominal segment.
