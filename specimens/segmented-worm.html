Freshwater Segmented Worm

Sensitivity Rating Very Tolerant
Phylum Annelida
Class Oligochaeta
Families Seven families
Size 1—30mm
Habitat Almost all water bodies
Diet Detritivorous and carnivorous – small pieces of dead and decomposing plants; animals including other invertebrates
Features Usually red or brown; stiff, needle-like structures (setae) located on the top and bottom of each of the body segments; similar to earthworms in appearance and movement
