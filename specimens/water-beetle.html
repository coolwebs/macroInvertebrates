Creeping Water Beetle

Sensitivity Rating Very Tolerant
Phylum Anthropoda
Class Insecta
Order Trichoptera
Family Naucoridae
Genera Naucoris
Size Less than 11mm
Habitat Inhabit still and slow-flowing waters
Diet Carnivorous – Catch and hold their prey with their raptorial legs
Features Flattened oval body and head. Their forelegs are modified for grasping with robust femurs and simplified tibiae and tarsi for holding the body of their prey. Most species have a striped pattern along their sides.

Gooderham. J. & Tsyrlin. E., (2002)
The waterbug book: a guide to freshwater macroinvertebrates
