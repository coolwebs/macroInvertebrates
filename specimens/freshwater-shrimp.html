Freshwater Shrimp

Sensitivity Rating Tolerant
Phylum Arthropoda
Class Crustacea
Order Decapoda
Family Atyidae and Palaemonidae
Size Up to 65mm
Habitat Lowland rivers, streams and standing fresh waters
Diet Omnivorous – animal and plant material
Features Fanned tail. Shrimp: bumpy appearance of rostrum; similar first two pairs of legs with tufts of long setae on their tips; crawl over creek bed, or beat abdomen to swim rapidly backwards. Prawn: elongated second pair of legs
