Back swimmer

Sensitivity Rating Very Tolerant
Phylum Anthropoda
Class Insecta
Order Hemiptera
Family Notonectidae
Genera Anisops, Enithares
Size Up to 11mm
Habitat Ponds, farm dams, and pools in slow-flowing streams
Diet Carnivorous (piercers) – extract body juices of other insects, tadpoles and small fish through pointed sucking mouth parts
Features Large eyes to assist with catching prey; elongated body; swim on their backs.
